🔒 Privacy

×

Your data stays on your device

Everything you log in Fauna — cycle data, meals, symptoms, mood — is stored locally on your device. It is not sent to any server unless you explicitly choose to back it up.

Why logging in matters

Right now Fauna works without an account. If you create an account (with email), your data gets backed up securely — so if you get a new phone, your entire history comes with you. Without an account, data lives only on this device.

Wearable data

When you connect Apple Health or Garmin, Fauna reads data from those apps — it never writes to them or shares your data with them. The connection is one-way, read-only.

Health data is sensitive

Menstrual and health data is among the most personal data that exists. Fauna will never sell it, share it with advertisers, or use it to target you. When a full account system is built, it will be end-to-end encrypted.

Photos you upload

Meal photos are processed locally when possible. In a future version with AI vision analysis, photos would be sent to an AI model for food recognition — you will always be asked before that happens, and photos are never stored permanently.

Your right to delete

You can delete all your data at any time. When the account system is live, one button wipes everything from every device and every server — permanently, no questions asked.
